# Checklist 07: Ongoing Compliance Obligations

## 7.1 Regulatory Reporting
- [ ] Form D annual amendment filed (US Reg D issuers)
- [ ] Periodic reports to investors (per offering documents)
- [ ] MiCA periodic reports to competent authority (EU ARTs)
- [ ] FATCA / CRS reporting completed (as applicable)

## 7.2 Investor Communications
- [ ] Annual report / NAV update delivered to investors
- [ ] Material events disclosed promptly (per subscription agreement)
- [ ] Redemption notices processed within required timeframe
- [ ] Investor contact information kept current

## 7.3 KYC/AML Refresh
- [ ] Annual KYC refresh triggered for all active investors
- [ ] Expired claims renewed and updated in ONCHAINID registry
- [ ] Ongoing sanctions / PEP screening running
- [ ] Investors with failed refresh flagged and transfer rights suspended pending resolution

## 7.4 Smart Contract Maintenance
- [ ] Compliance module updates reviewed by legal and technical teams
- [ ] Upgrades tested on testnet before mainnet deployment
- [ ] Upgrade governance process followed (multi-sig, timelock if applicable)
- [ ] Post-upgrade transfer restriction testing completed

## 7.5 Supervisory Review
- [ ] Monthly: transaction blotter reviewed [FINRA 3110]
- [ ] Quarterly: compliance module configuration reviewed
- [ ] Annually: full supervisory procedures review
- [ ] Annually: external compliance audit completed

## 7.6 Regulatory Monitoring
- [ ] Regulatory update subscription active (SEC, FINRA, MiCA, FATF)
- [ ] Material regulatory changes assessed for impact on token structure
- [ ] Legal counsel briefed on significant regulatory developments
- [ ] India Asset Tokenisation Bill 2026 — monitor legislative progress
