# Checklist 04: Investor Onboarding

## 4.1 Eligibility Determination
- [ ] Investor type determined:
  - [ ] Accredited investor (US — Reg D)
  - [ ] Qualified purchaser (US — Section 3(c)(7))
  - [ ] Non-US person (Reg S)
  - [ ] Professional investor (MiCA / MiFID II)
  - [ ] Retail investor (if offering permits)
- [ ] Eligibility determination documented and retained

## 4.2 Investor Documentation
- [ ] Subscription agreement executed
- [ ] Investor questionnaire completed
- [ ] Accreditation verification obtained (third-party or self-certification)
- [ ] Source of funds declaration obtained (for investments > threshold)
- [ ] W-8BEN or W-9 obtained (US tax purposes)

## 4.3 Disclosure Delivery
- [ ] Offering memorandum / private placement memorandum (PPM) delivered
- [ ] Risk factors disclosed in writing
- [ ] MiCA white paper delivered (EU investors)
- [ ] Investor acknowledgment of receipt obtained

## 4.4 ONCHAINID Claim Issuance
- [ ] Investor wallet address registered in ONCHAINID registry
- [ ] Required claims issued:
  - [ ] KYC verified claim
  - [ ] Investor type / accreditation claim
  - [ ] Country claim (ISO 3166-1 alpha-2)
  - [ ] AML cleared claim
- [ ] Claim expiry dates confirmed
- [ ] Investor notified of wallet address registration

## 4.5 Post-Onboarding
- [ ] Investor added to cap table / register of members
- [ ] Token allocation confirmed
- [ ] Onboarding record archived
